Face Cleansing Wipes

Our face wipes are made to be single use and can be discarded into a compost or regular garbage. In general face cleansing wipes are abrasive to exfoliate, but ours are gentle and make a large difference.

How to Use

The face cleansing wipes come in a small compressed tablet that is about the size of a quarter and need to be activated. To active the wipe just add water! When water is added a foamy cleansing solution will form.

How to Store

We would suggest that you keep these tablets in a moisture free zone in the container they come in. As moisture would most likely ruining the compression of the tablet and possibly create growth of unwanted bacteria.
